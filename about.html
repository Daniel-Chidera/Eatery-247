About Eatery 24/7

Where Culinary Excellence Meets Convenience

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Eatery 24/7 was born from a simple belief: exceptional dining shouldn't be confined to traditional hours. We understand that cravings don't follow a schedule, and neither should quality food.

What started as a small kitchen serving late-night travelers has grown into a beloved destination for food enthusiasts across the city. Our commitment to authentic Nigerian cuisine, prepared with love and served with warmth, has remained unchanged since day one.

Today, we're proud to serve our community around the clock, bringing the rich flavors of Nigerian heritage to your table whenever hunger strikes.

Our Mission

To provide authentic Nigerian cuisine with unwavering quality and service, accessible 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We strive to make every meal an experience that celebrates our culinary heritage while meeting the needs of modern life.

Our Vision

To become the leading destination for Nigerian cuisine, setting the standard for quality, authenticity, and convenience. We envision a future where traditional flavors are celebrated and accessible to everyone, anytime.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Authenticity

We stay true to traditional Nigerian recipes while adapting to modern tastes, ensuring every dish tells a story of our rich culinary heritage.

Quality

From sourcing the finest ingredients to meticulous preparation, we never compromise on quality. Every dish that leaves our kitchen meets our high standards.

Service

Our customers are family. We're committed to providing warm, attentive service that makes every visit memorable and every meal enjoyable.

Sustainability

We believe in responsible sourcing and minimizing our environmental impact. Supporting local farmers and sustainable practices is part of who we are.

Innovation

While honoring tradition, we embrace innovation in our processes and service delivery, always looking for ways to serve you better.

Community

We're more than a restaurant; we're a gathering place. Building connections and fostering community is at the heart of what we do.

Why Choose Eatery 24/7?

24/7 Availability

Never worry about timing. Whether it's breakfast at dawn, dinner at midnight, or a late-night snack, we're always open.

Diverse Menu
